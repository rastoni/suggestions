CHANIA AREA

Thirty minutes away for Kissamos you will find the picturesque city of Chania. The main attraction is of course the old town and the Venetian harbor, where you can enjoy your coffee and walk around the narrow roads and visit the local shops

OTHER SUGGESTIONS

In the Southern part of Crete you can find plenty of beaches different than the ones in the Nothern part. Some of them are Sougia, Gialiskari, Fragkokastello all near the town of Palaiochora. You can also explore the southern part of Rethymno where you can enjoy the beaches of Preveli (with a beautiful palm forest), Agios Pavlos, Kalipso, Damnoni, Plakias, Schinaria and many more

USING THE SAFE

In order to use the safe inside your room please follow these directions. Please ensure that by the time you check in the safe door is open. In case the safe door is closed make sure to contact us.

  • With the safe door open, press the red button on the inside of the safe. A SET CODE message will appear on the outside screen
  • Type your code between 3 and 8 digits and press the START button. You will hear a beeping sound, a message on the screen IN and the digits you have selected. This means that you have successfully entered your code
  • Please keep in mind that the safe lock is manual, which means that you have to turn the lever on the left in order to close and open it after you enter your code
  • In case that you forget your safe code, please contact us
